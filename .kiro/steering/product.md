# ReadMyFinePrint Product Overview

ReadMyFinePrint is a **privacy-first AI-powered contract analysis platform** that transforms complex legal documents into accessible summaries while maintaining enterprise-grade security.

## Core Product
- **Service**: AI-powered legal document analysis using GPT-4o
- **Target Users**: Legal professionals, businesses, individuals reviewing contracts
- **Key Value**: Simplifies complex legal language while protecting sensitive information

## Privacy & Security Focus
- **Privacy-First Architecture**: Session-based processing with automatic PII detection and redaction
- **Enterprise Security**: Military-grade security with complete audit trails
- **Compliance**: GDPR, CCPA, and PIPEDA compliant with SOC 2 Type II controls

## Business Model
- **Subscription Tiers**: Multiple pricing tiers with different AI models and limits
- **Stripe Integration**: Payment processing and subscription management
- **Usage Tracking**: Document analysis limits and token usage monitoring

## Key Features
- Multi-format document support (PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT)
- Automatic PII detection and redaction
- Risk assessment and analysis
- Multi-device secure authentication
- Admin dashboard and user management
- Blog system for content marketing
